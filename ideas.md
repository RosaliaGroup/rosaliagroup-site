## Idea A: "Urban Warmth" — Brutalist Warmth meets Organic Modernism

**Design Movement:** Warm Brutalism / Organic Modernism

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw architectural confidence — bold, unapologetic typography with structural weight
2. Warm earth tones to humanize the urban real estate context
3. Asymmetric, editorial-style layouts that feel like a curated magazine spread
4. Tactile texture: subtle grain overlays, rough paper-like backgrounds

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: warm off-white (#F5F0E8) — like aged parchment, grounding and trustworthy
- Primary: deep terracotta (#C4622D) — warmth, ambition, woman-owned energy
- Accent: muted olive green (#7A8C5E) — growth, NJ landscape
- Dark: charcoal slate (#2C2C2C) — architectural gravitas

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Horizontal scrolling hero with overlapping text blocks
- Staggered card grids with irregular column widths (2-3-2 rhythm)
- Full-bleed section breaks with diagonal clip-paths

**Signature Elements:**
1. Large oversized numerals as decorative anchors (200+, 98%, 10+)
2. Thin rule lines separating sections like architectural drawings
3. Serif display headings (Playfair Display) paired with mono-spaced labels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover reveals: property cards lift with shadow on hover, revealing price overlay
- Parallax scrolling on hero background images
- Smooth section transitions with slide-up entrance animations

**Animation:**
- Entrance: text lines stagger-slide from bottom (0.1s delay each)
- Cards: scale(1.02) + shadow-lg on hover with 200ms ease
- Nav: underline draw animation on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (bold, italic for emphasis)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, modern, readable)
- Labels: Space Mono (uppercase, letter-spaced, architectural feel)

## Idea B: "Midnight Luxury" — Dark Editorial Real Estate

**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury / High-End Editorial

**Core Principles:**
1. Dark, sophisticated palette that positions Rosalia as premium
2. Gold and champagne accents for aspirational real estate feel
3. Full-width cinematic imagery with dramatic overlays
4. Minimal text, maximum impact — let properties speak

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black (#0F0F0F) — premium, cinematic
- Primary: champagne gold (#C9A84C) — luxury, aspiration
- Secondary: warm white (#F2EDE4) — contrast, readability
- Accent: deep forest green (#1E3A2F) — NJ nature, stability

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Vertical scroll with full-viewport sections
- Split-screen layouts (image left, text right)
- Floating property cards with glass-morphism effect

**Signature Elements:**
1. Gold horizontal rule dividers
2. Property cards with frosted glass overlay on dark images
3. Animated counter numbers in gold

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Immersive scroll experience
- Property image zoom on hover
- Smooth page transitions

**Animation:**
- Hero: slow Ken Burns effect on background image
- Section reveal: fade-in from opacity 0 with slight Y translate
- Gold accents: shimmer animation on load

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (elegant, high-fashion)
- Body: Outfit (clean, modern)
- Labels: Montserrat (uppercase, tracked)

## Idea C: "Clean Authority" — Modern Professional with Bold Contrast

**Design Movement:** Swiss International Style meets Contemporary Real Estate

**Core Principles:**
1. Crisp, high-contrast design that communicates trust and expertise
2. Strong typographic hierarchy — size and weight do the heavy lifting
3. Generous whitespace with intentional asymmetry
4. Data-forward: statistics and social proof prominently displayed

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: pure white (#FFFFFF) with warm cream sections (#FAF8F5)
- Primary: deep navy (#1A2B4A) — authority, trust, professional
- Accent: vibrant coral (#E8533A) — energy, action, woman-owned boldness
- Neutral: warm gray (#8C8C8C) — supporting text

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Left-anchored hero with large typography bleeding off-screen
- Horizontal scrolling property showcase
- Alternating left/right content blocks for services

**Signature Elements:**
1. Bold oversized section labels rotated 90° as vertical text
2. Thick left-border accent lines on feature cards
3. Property cards with bottom-anchored price tags

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Crisp, snappy interactions — no excessive animation
- Clear visual feedback on all interactive elements
- Tabbed interfaces for service categories

**Animation:**
- Entrance: clean fade + translate-Y(20px) → 0 on scroll
- Buttons: background fill from left on hover
- Numbers: count-up animation when in viewport

**Typography System:**
- Display: Fraunces (variable, expressive serif)
- Body: Inter (reliable, readable)
- Labels: IBM Plex Mono (technical credibility)

## Selected Approach: **Idea A — "Urban Warmth"**

Warm Brutalism with editorial asymmetry. This approach:
- Differentiates Rosalia Group from generic real estate sites
- Conveys warmth and approachability (woman-run, family business)
- Maintains architectural confidence appropriate for property management
- The terracotta + olive palette is distinctive and memorable
